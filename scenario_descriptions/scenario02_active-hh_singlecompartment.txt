Scenario 02: Responses of HH-type cell to step current injection
=================================================================

Create a single compartment neuron with area 'A',  with passive leak 
channels density 'glk' and capacitance 'C'. Inject with step current 'I' from 
t=100 until t=250. Record voltage from t=0 to t=350

Vary: 
 - A: 1000um2, 1600um2
 - C: 1uF/cm2, 2.0uF/cm2
 - I: 120pA, 200pA
 - glk: 0.3mS/cm2, 1.3mS/cm2


Filename-Format: scenario2__A1000_C1_I120_GLK0.3_output.txt
